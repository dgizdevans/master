# Support Materials

Supplementary materials related to the master's thesis research process.

## Contents

**`bibliometrics_literature_analysis.ipynb`** — Bibliometric analysis of the
literature base assembled for this thesis. Covers 299 papers sourced from the
Zotero group library: [TSI ST Group Library](https://www.zotero.org/groups/6340306/st/library).
Includes tag-based classification, publication year distribution, and
source analysis.
